How it works

1. Identify a Recipient

Create a free Chesed Train page for a friend who could use some support.

2. Enter Dates

Select the dates when meals would be helpful

3. Provide Preferences

Provide the recipient's meal preferences, allergies, and ideal drop-off time.

4. Share

Share with friends, family, and neighbors so they can sign up to provide meals.

Who It's For

Chesed Train is for everyone who could use some help during common life events:

  • Arrival of a new baby
  • Simchas / Group Events
  • Injuries / Surgery
  • Extended Illnesses / Hospital Stays
  • Shiva Houses
